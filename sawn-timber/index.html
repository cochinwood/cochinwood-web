Questions buyers ask

Sawn timber, answered.

Which timber species do you supply?

Rubberwood, eucalyptus, acacia, mahogany, jackwood and silverwood as standard, plus specialty species such as teak, rosewood and sheesham to order.

Is the timber kiln-dried?

Yes — kiln-dried to a controlled moisture content and squared, so it stays stable through finishing and shipping.

Can you cut to custom sizes?

Yes — logs, beams, planks or finished sections sawn to your cutting list.

What is the minimum order?

By the container load (FCL), FOB Cochin Port, with export documentation at dispatch.
