Plywood Supply to Jamshedpur

Cochin Wood Industries

Why Jamshedpur is a serious plywood town

Jamshedpur is a planned steel city, and almost everything that moves out of it moves on wood. Tata Steel's flat and long products, Tata Motors' commercial vehicle aggregates, TRF's bulk material-handling equipment, Tata Cummins engines, and a deep ecosystem of tier-1 and tier-2 suppliers in the Adityapur belt all share a common downstream problem: how to box, brace and skid heavy industrial goods for road, rail and ocean transit without movement or damage. That problem is solved with packing-grade plywood, hardwood pallets, ISPM-15 crates and container flooring. The Cochin Wood group has been making plywood and sawn-timber products in Perumbavoor since 1986, and Jamshedpur is one of the eastern-belt destinations our packing range routinely heads to.

The Jamshedpur industrial picture

To size up packing demand from this city, it helps to look at the geography. Jamshedpur sits in the East Singhbhum district of Jharkhand, with industrial activity clustered in a few clearly identifiable zones.

The Tata works core

The original Tata Steel plant inside the city, plus the cold-rolling and tube-making units nearby, are anchors. Steel pipes, tubes, plates, coils and structurals leaving these mills need either heavy-duty open-top crates with wooden bolster bases or skid pallets sized to the coil cradle. Plywood here is mostly for end caps, dunnage and skid tops rather than full-box construction.

Adityapur Industrial Area (AIA)

Across the Kharkai river, AIA is one of the larger industrial estates in eastern India and the densest source of packing-plywood demand in the region. Auto-component makers, casting and forging units, machine shops, hydraulic and pneumatic suppliers and dozens of Tata Motors vendors operate from AIA's Phase I through Phase VIII. Their typical packing brief: small-to-medium wooden boxes built on a plywood base and cap, sized for 4 to 8 units per crate, sometimes returnable, often export-marked for Africa, the Gulf and Southeast Asia.

Gamharia, Jugsalai and the rail belt

Gamharia hosts heavier engineering — rolling-stock components, wagon parts, and bulk material handling gear. Crates here are larger and structural; the plywood spec moves from 6mm liners to 12mm and 18mm side panels capable of taking lashing point loads. Jugsalai and the inland container depot at Tatanagar handle the rail-leg outbound; container-flooring grade plywood shows up here for reefer and dry-box refurbishment.

What we ship to Jamshedpur

Packing-grade plywood — the workhorse

Our commercial / rubberwood packing plywood is the most-quoted line into Jharkhand. Standard thicknesses cover the full crate stack: 6mm and 8mm for top liners and division panels, 10mm and 12mm for side walls of medium crates, 15mm and 18mm for base panels and heavy-duty side walls. Sheets are made to a 1220 x 2440 mm (4' x 8') base, with custom-cut sizes available for repeat-order box makers who want zero offcuts.

Okoume face plywood — when the box has to look the part

For export crates that go through customs handling and sit on a buyer's loading dock under their brand mark, an Okoume face gives a cleaner finish, fewer surface defects and accepts stencil markings well. We supply Okoume-faced packing plywood as a parallel SKU; the cost premium is real, so it is usually specified only on top-tier export consignments.

Hardwood pallets and runners

For the AIA auto-component flow, jackwood and rubberwood pallet runners are the more common ask than full plywood pallets. Standard footprints we ship: 1200 x 800 mm (Euro), 1200 x 1000 mm (industrial), and 1100 x 1100 mm for the Southeast-Asia container fit. Block-style and stringer-style both available. Jackwood runners are quoted at Rs 400 / cft (with the standard quarter-inch-minus tolerance).

Container flooring grade

For container builders and refurbishment shops near the Tatanagar ICD, we supply 28mm container-floor plywood with the keruing/apitong-style hardwood face, hot-pressed and phenolic-bonded. This is a narrower spec — quote on request rather than off-the-shelf.

Shuttering plywood — the secondary line

Jamshedpur's infrastructure projects (steel-plant expansions, road overbridges around Mango and Bistupur, residential builds on the Adityapur side) consume film-faced shuttering plywood. We carry the standard 12mm and 18mm BWP-bonded shuttering ply for site contractors.

Kochi-to-Jamshedpur logistics

The road distance from our Perumbavoor (Ernakulam) loading point to Jamshedpur is approximately 1,900 to 2,000 km depending on which national-highway combination is chosen. The realistic transit lane runs Kochi to Bangalore on NH-544 / NH-44, then NH-44 north through Hyderabad and Nagpur, before turning east onto NH-49 and NH-33 through Raipur and Ranchi into Jamshedpur. Transit time for a full-truck load is typically 6 to 8 days by road, depending on driver shifts and seasonal road conditions in the central belt.

For sea-leg economics on heavier volumes, the alternative is Cochin Port to Haldia or Paradip, then a short inland leg to Jamshedpur. This is rarely cheaper for plywood — coastal shipping windows are long and the inland transfer eats the saving — so road remains the default lane. We size loads to 32-feet single-axle or multi-axle trailers; a typical packing-plywood truck carries around 18 to 22 tonnes depending on thickness mix.

FAQ

What is your typical lead time to Jamshedpur on a full-truck order?

For stocked thicknesses in commercial packing grade, around 7 to 10 days from PI confirmation — that covers production scheduling at the contract factory, loading at Perumbavoor, and the 6 to 8 day road transit to Adityapur or central Jamshedpur. Okoume-faced and custom-cut sizes add 3 to 5 days.

Do you supply ISPM-15 stamped pallets and crates?

We supply ISPM-15 compliant packing-grade plywood and hardwood pallet components. The actual ISPM-15 heat-treatment stamp is applied by registered packers at the box-assembly stage. For exporters who need the full assembled, stamped crate, we coordinate with treatment partners on request.

How are prices quoted — and what is included?

All rates are quoted ex-factory Perumbavoor. GST and transport are extra. Once the thickness mix, monthly quantity and delivery address are confirmed, a proforma invoice with the all-in landed Jamshedpur price is issued. For repeat consignees we set up monthly rate cards.
