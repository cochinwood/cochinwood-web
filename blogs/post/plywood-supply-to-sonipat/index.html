Plywood Supply to Sonipat

Cochin Wood Industries

Sonipat sits at the northern edge of the National Capital Region, an hour up the GT Road from Delhi and the kind of district where heavy engineering, cycles, tyres and steel all share the same trucking lane. For a plywood supplier shipping out of Kochi, that mix is exactly the customer profile we are built for: factories that crate finished goods, palletise sub-assemblies, and floor export containers month after month. This page lays out what the Cochin Wood group ships into Sonipat, how the lane works from a Kerala port, and the decision rules that decide which grade of plywood ends up under your load.

Why Sonipat's industrial belt matters for packing plywood

Sonipat is not one industrial cluster but several stitched together. The Kundli-Manesar-Palwal expressway opened the western flank of the district to large-format manufacturing, and the older Rai and Kundli HSIIDC estates have hosted bicycle, auto-component, food-processing, textile and pharmaceutical units for decades. North of the city, the Barhi industrial area handles smaller fabrication and ancillary work. Add the Tata Steel BSL plant at Sahibabad-Sonipat axis and the cycle and tyre majors with operations in and around the district, and the packaging demand profile becomes clear: thousands of export-bound CKD crates, heavy steel coils that need dunnage and floor boards, and palletised cargo destined for ICDs at Tughlakabad, Garhi Harsaru and Patparganj before sea-leg.

For a factory crating bicycles in CKD form, every shipment is an export carton waiting to be skid-loaded into a 40-foot container. For a tyre plant moving stacks of casings, the load needs ply floors that survive forklift abuse. For an auto-component shop sending sub-assemblies to OEMs, returnable pallets and one-way export pallets sit on the same shop floor. Each of these flows is a separate plywood SKU conversation, and Sonipat buyers tend to mix them inside one annual contract.

The Sonipat industrial picture in detail

Kundli and Rai HSIIDC estates

Kundli's planned plots host bicycle and component manufacturers, packaging converters and pharma units. Rai is the older estate with a heavier engineering footprint. Both estates feed plywood demand from the same vendors: case-makers and pallet shops operating inside or just outside the gate, building to the OEM's drawing.

Barhi and Murthal industrial pockets

North of NH-44, Barhi and Murthal carry smaller fabrication units, food and dairy processors, and a growing cluster of warehousing and 3PL operations along the expressway. Warehousing demand tends to favour standard ISO pallets and one-way export skids rather than custom crates.

Cycle, tyre and steel sectors

Sonipat's bicycle sector ships CKD export crates with ply walls and battened skid bases. The tyre sector around the district moves casings on ply-floored skids and uses heavier-gauge plywood for export crate sides. Steel and steel-product flows need thicker shuttering or container-floor grade plywood that can carry coil weights without delamination at the edges.

What we ship into Sonipat

Three product families cover almost every load that leaves Sonipat under a CWI-stamped board.

Packing-grade plywood sheets

Our MR-Packing range runs 6 mm through 18 mm in the standard 8x4 sheet, with 9 mm, 12 mm and 18 mm doing the bulk of the case-making volume. The 6 mm and 8 mm sheets handle inner partitions and lid panels for lighter loads. The 12 mm and 18 mm sheets carry side walls and bases on heavier crates. Sheets ship strapped in mill bundles, suitable for both forklift and crane offload at a Sonipat dock.

Export crates and packing cases

When a customer prefers finished crates over sheets, we cut, batten and bolt to drawing in Kerala and ship the assembled or knock-down case. CKD bicycle crates, tyre stack crates, machinery and engineering-good cases all sit in this lane. Heat-treatment to ISPM-15 is available where the destination port demands it; the IPPC stamp goes onto the same batten.

Wooden pallets

Standard four-way entry pallets in 1200x1000 mm and 1200x800 mm sizes, plus custom skid-bases sized to the load. For warehousing customers in Murthal and along the expressway, the standard ISO sizes are the high-runner; for OEM-bound auto-component shipments, custom sizes built to the cage or rack are more common.

Container flooring and dunnage

For steel coils, machinery and heavy export, container-floor grade plywood at 18 mm or above acts as the load-bearing floor over the existing container floor, distributing point loads and protecting the box. Dunnage strips and chocks ship from the same family of timber.

Kochi-to-Sonipat: how the lane runs

Kochi to Sonipat is roughly 2,300 km by road, four to six days transit on the standard trunk route through Bengaluru, Hyderabad and Nagpur and into the NCR via the Agra-Faridabad approach. For sheet plywood and pre-cut components, the road option works because the load is dense and stacks well in a 32-foot or 40-foot truck.

For larger volumes, the rail-and-road combination via ICD Tughlakabad makes more sense: Cochin Port to JNPT or Mundra, ICD rake to Tughlakabad, last-mile truck to Sonipat. The maths depends on monthly volume and the customer's willingness to break stock into ICD-pickup batches. Where the load is mixed plywood plus finished crates, we route the crates by direct truck and palletise the sheets for the rail leg.

Decision rules: which grade for which Sonipat load

  • Packing-grade MR plywood is the default for export crates carrying bicycles, tyres, auto-components and general engineering goods. It is the most cost-efficient board for one-way export packaging.
  • BWR/BWP grades earn their cost when the cargo is high-value, the sea voyage is long, or the customer ships into humid coastal destinations through the monsoon. For Sonipat buyers exporting to GCC, Africa or South-East Asia, BWR makes sense on premium SKUs.
  • Okoume-faced packing ply is a face-veneer option on the MR-Packing and BWR grades. It is the right call when the buyer specifies a light, uniform face for branded crates or when the destination customer rejects darker veneer faces.
  • Shuttering plywood is the wrong choice for export packing but the right choice for construction sites and infrastructure project buyers in the district. Different grade, different conversation.

FAQ

Can you ship directly to Kundli or Rai HSIIDC plot addresses?

Yes. Trucks deliver to the plant gate on standard HSIIDC plots; the only check is unloading access for a 32-foot or 40-foot trailer. Crane-offload is available on request for pre-assembled crates above 1.5 tonnes.

What is the minimum order for a Sonipat consignment?

Full-truck loads are the most economical, typically 18-22 tonnes per truck depending on sheet thickness. Below that, we consolidate with other northern dispatches or route via ICD Tughlakabad for buyers willing to take ICD pickup.

Can crates ship pre-stamped with ISPM-15 for export?

Yes. Heat-treatment under IPPC certification is available on the batten timber, with the IS PM-15 stamp on the finished crate. Specify on the PO whether your destination port requires the stamp.
