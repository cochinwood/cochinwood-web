Plywood Supply to Pithampur

Cochin Wood Industries

Pithampur — the Detroit of Central India — runs on metal that has to travel. Commercial vehicle chassis, agricultural machinery, diesel engines, and tier-1 castings move out of this Madhya Pradesh industrial belt every day, and almost all of them ride inside a wooden case, on a wooden pallet, or over a plywood-floored container. That is the gap the Cochin Wood group fills. We are a plywood manufacturer based in Kochi, Kerala, operating through contract factories under CWI specifications, and we supply packing-grade plywood, ready crates, pallets and container flooring into the Pithampur–Indore industrial corridor.

Why Pithampur is a serious plywood-consuming belt

Pithampur sits about 30 km southwest of Indore on NH-3, and is run as a planned cluster by MPAKVN (Madhya Pradesh Audyogik Kendra Vikas Nigam, Indore). The town is laid out in four developed sectors — Sector 1, Sector 2, Sector 3 and the newer Sector 7 — plus the MPSEZ at Pithampur Special Economic Zone. The mix of tenants here is what makes the packaging demand interesting: heavy commercial vehicles, tractors, off-road equipment, engines, auto components, pharma capital equipment and food-processing machinery.

The industrial sub-belts that drive plywood demand

  • Commercial vehicle and tractor OEMs — Mahindra & Mahindra's truck and bus division, Volvo Eicher Commercial Vehicles, and John Deere all run major plants in Pithampur. CKD (Completely Knocked Down) and SKD shipments of chassis aggregates and tractor assemblies are crated for export and inter-plant movement.
  • Diesel engines and power systems — Cummins India operates in the cluster, and engines plus large genset enclosures need heavy-duty crates with rebated corners and lifting skids.
  • Tier-1 auto components — castings, forgings, transmissions, axles, sheet-metal panels and bumpers. These move on pallets between Pithampur and assembly plants in Pune, Chennai, Lucknow and overseas.
  • Pharma and food-processing capital equipment — Pithampur and the wider Indore region also house pharma machinery and food-machinery makers. Their finished equipment travels in fumigated, ISPM-15 compliant cases.
  • Container freight stations and 3PL hubs — the Dhannad and Mangliya ICDs near Indore, plus container freight stations around Pithampur, generate steady demand for 14 mm and 18 mm container flooring and side liners.

Wooden cases and CKD crates

For the heavy commercial vehicle and tractor lines in Sector 1 and Sector 3, the typical case is a plywood-clad pinewood or hardwood frame, with 12 mm or 15 mm packing ply skin, runners on the base, and ISPM-15 stamped softwood for any solid-wood members. We build to drawing — load weight, lashing points, fork-pocket spacing, internal cradles and foam profiling. Where the OEM has a packaging engineer's spec sheet, we work directly off that.

Pallets

Common pallet sizes for the Pithampur auto belt:

  • 1200 x 1000 mm — the ISO/euro-adjacent standard for component movement.
  • 1200 x 800 mm — Euro pallet, where the consignee is in Europe.
  • 1100 x 1100 mm — common for Japanese and South-East Asian OEM lines.
  • Custom oversized skids — for engine blocks, transmissions and chassis sub-assemblies.

Container flooring and CFS work

18 mm BWR plywood with phenolic glue is the working spec for container re-flooring jobs at the ICDs around Indore. We supply sheet stock or pre-cut planks to the CFS contractors.

Kochi to Pithampur — the logistics picture

Pithampur is roughly 1,650–1,700 km by road from our Perumbavoor base near Kochi. Trucks run via NH-544 / NH-44 through Salem, Bengaluru bypass, Hubballi, Pune, then NH-3 / NH-52 through Dhule and Indore to Pithampur. Single-driver transit is typically 4–5 days, double-driver 3 days for time-critical loads.

For most packing-grade plywood orders the economics favour direct road from Perumbavoor — a 32-foot multi-axle SXL carries roughly 22–24 tonnes of plywood, which is around 950–1,050 sheets of 12 mm depending on density. Where the customer wants a smaller drop and the freight per sheet is sensitive, we consolidate loads to Indore CFS and arrange last-mile drops to Sector 1, 2, 3 or 7. For Okoume export-grade lots, we can also ship FOB Cochin if the consignee is moving the finished crate overseas from a different port — that decision is usually driven by who controls the export leg.

Which grade for which Pithampur load — decision rules

Use MR Packing grade when

  • The case is moving domestic — Pune, Chennai, Lucknow, Jamshedpur.
  • The voyage, if any, is short (UAE, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh).
  • Cost per sheet matters and the case will be opened once and discarded.

Use BWR / Phenolic-bonded plywood when

  • The crate is going on a long sea voyage — Europe, US, West Africa, Latin America.
  • The case will sit on a port apron or open yard for extended periods.
  • Container re-flooring or repeated-use cradles.

Use Okoume face when

  • The end customer is overseas and case appearance is part of brand presentation — premium agricultural machinery, defence equipment, high-end engines.
  • Light-weight case construction is preferred for air freight or weight-sensitive sea freight.

Use shuttering ply when

  • The buyer is a civil contractor working on a Pithampur factory shed, MPAKVN infrastructure or warehouse expansion — not a packaging use, but a recurring Pithampur demand we serve from the same stock.

FAQ

How much plywood fits in one truck from Kochi to Pithampur?

A 32-foot SXL carries around 22–24 tonnes. For 12 mm packing-grade plywood that translates to roughly 950–1,050 sheets of 8' x 4'. For 18 mm container flooring the count drops to around 640–700 sheets at the same weight.

Can you supply ISPM-15 stamped wooden cases ready to ship?

Yes. The plywood skin is BIS-grade; the solid-wood frame and runners are heat-treated and ISPM-15 stamped before despatch. We co-ordinate the HT treatment through approved facilities and send the case to the OEM's stuffing yard in Pithampur ready to load.

Do you take partial loads or only full trucks?

Both. Full truckload (FTL) from Perumbavoor direct to Pithampur is the cheapest per sheet. For smaller orders we consolidate at the Indore CFS hub and last-mile to the consignee, or piggyback on an existing Pithampur load — ask when you request the quote.
