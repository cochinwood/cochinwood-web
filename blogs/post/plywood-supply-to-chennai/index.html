Plywood Supply to Chennai

Cochin Wood Industries

Plywood for Chennai's auto exports, EV battery lines and SEZ electronics.

Chennai is South Asia's auto capital — around 30% of India's auto output. The Sriperumbudur-Oragadam corridor is the densest OEM cluster in South India, hosting multiple global passenger-car, commercial-vehicle and motorcycle plants. Add the iPhone assembly line at Sriperumbudur, the city's 2025 EV-battery plant, and the emerging Ola/Ather/TVS EV cluster — and Chennai needs crates that match SEZ paperwork as well as inland transit. We supply Kochi-origin plywood and crating into that market, 689 km up NH-544/NH-44.

Why source from Kochi

Packaging that feeds the Oragadam-Sriperumbudur auto corridor.

Chennai's auto OEMs at Oragadam and Sriperumbudur crate CKD/SKD parts daily for export to North America, Europe and ASEAN. EV battery packs from the city's battery plant and from the wider EV cluster need Class-9 dangerous-goods crates with cushioning. Sriperumbudur SEZ's electronics assemblers need splinter-free, SEZ-compatible pallets. Defence and aerospace tier-1s feeding BEL and HAL need MIL-spec moisture-barrier crates. We supply Kochi-origin plywood into the Chennai belt — and we are familiar with SEZ delivery challans, Form-I and zero-rated IGST invoicing for Sriperumbudur and Oragadam SEZ deliveries.

Logistics

Kochi to Chennai — 689 km, one-day truck transit via Salem.

NH-544 from Kochi to Salem (~362 km, five hours), then NH-44/NH-48 from Salem to Chennai through Krishnagiri-Vellore (~342 km, five hours). Total: 10-12 hours of driving, 18-22 hours of truck transit with halts. Loads dispatched in the morning are unloading in Sriperumbudur or Oragadam the next morning. Walayar border check (KL-TN) is the only paperwork stop — e-way bill and GST documentation ready, usually under 30 minutes. For SEZ exporters we supply ISPM-15 heat-treated, IPPC-stamped wood with phytosanitary documentation, original certificates released to verified buyers, and we are familiar with SEZ delivery challans, Form-I and zero-rated IGST invoicing. Share destination port (Chennai, Kattupalli, Ennore) and monthly volume.

Chennai FAQ

Common questions from Chennai buyers

Can you supply into Sriperumbudur or Oragadam SEZs?

Yes. SEZ delivery challans, Form-I, GSTIN-LUT and zero-rated IGST invoicing are standard for us. Share your unit's SEZ code and consignee details.

Do you supply UN-spec crates for EV battery exports?

Yes. Class-9 dangerous-goods rated plywood crates with cushioning are part of our standard build for EV battery pack and cell shipments. Spec sheet on request.

Is the wood ISPM-15 compliant for export?

Export pallets and crates are heat-treated and IPPC-stamped to ISPM-15, with phytosanitary documentation for the destination country.

What is the minimum order?

We are a bulk supplier and quote on container or full-truck volumes. Share your monthly requirement and we will confirm terms.
