Plywood Supply to Bengaluru

Cochin Wood Industries

Bengaluru · Karnataka

Packing plywood for Bengaluru's aerospace, electronics and Peenya engineering belts.

Bengaluru is India's only city that runs an aerospace SEZ, a four-thousand-acre engineering MSME cluster, and a rising iPhone-assembly belt at the same time. The KIADB Aerospace Park at Devanahalli backs onto Kempegowda International; Peenya in the north-west is one of Asia's largest SME engineering estates; Bommasandra and Whitefield run electronics, biotech and component lines; Foxconn's 300-acre Devanahalli campus is bringing iPhone assembly into Karnataka. None of that ships through a Bengaluru port — it all routes through Chennai, Kattupalli or Cochin. Cochin is the closest at ~550 km. We supply Kochi-origin plywood and crating into that belt.

Aerospace & SEZ-grade · ICD Whitefield INWFD6 · Kochi → Bengaluru 550 km, 11-14 hr

Packaging supply for KIADB Aerospace Park, Peenya, Bommasandra and Whitefield.

Bengaluru's exporters run a wider grade-mix than most Indian cities. KIADB Aerospace Park needs custom moisture-controlled crates with internal cradles for fuselage subassemblies and aerospace components. Peenya's MSME engineering belt — 4,000+ acres, ~12,000 units — ships heavy machine parts, castings, instrumentation. Bommasandra-Jigani and Hoskote ship auto-component CKD/SKD. Whitefield SEZ electronics + biotech exporters palletise for FOB Chennai/Mumbai/Cochin. ICD Whitefield (CONCOR, INWFD6) is the primary rail-linked dry port. We hold the supply line from Kochi — 11-14 hours via NH-544/NH-75.

Kochi to Bengaluru — 550-600 km, 11-14 hours via NH-544/NH-75.

A 32-ft trailer dispatched at our Kochi yard reaches Bengaluru in 11-14 hours running — usually next-day delivery. The route goes Kochi-Salem on NH-544, then NH-44/NH-75 through Krishnagiri or Mysuru depending on destination cluster. KIADB Aerospace Park (Devanahalli) and the north Bengaluru belt are easiest via the Mysuru route; Hoskote, Whitefield and Bommasandra are easier via Krishnagiri. For exports we route to your facility, to ICD Whitefield (INWFD6), or onward to FOB Chennai, Kattupalli or Cochin. ISPM-15 heat-treated, IPPC-stamped wood with phytosanitary documentation; original certificates released to verified buyers on request.

Bengaluru FAQ: Common questions from Bengaluru buyers

Can you supply custom aerospace crates with internal cradles?

Yes. Custom-CAD plywood crates with internal cradles, sub-12% moisture, ISPM-15 stamped — suitable for fuselage subassemblies, aerostructure components and aerospace-SEZ deliveries. Send the drawing.

Can you deliver to ICD Whitefield (INWFD6)?

Yes — ICD Whitefield is a regular drop. Trucks dispatched from Kochi reach Bengaluru in 11-14 hours; stuffing can be timed to your rail schedule for Chennai or Cochin.

Is the wood ISPM-15 compliant for export?

Export pallets and crates are heat-treated and IPPC-stamped to ISPM-15, with phytosanitary documentation for the destination country.

What is the minimum order?

We are a bulk supplier and quote on container or full-truck volumes. Share your monthly requirement and we will confirm terms.

Shipping out of Devanahalli, Peenya or Whitefield?

Send spec, drawing if custom, and destination port — we will quote ex-Kochi with delivery to Bengaluru or ICD Whitefield.
