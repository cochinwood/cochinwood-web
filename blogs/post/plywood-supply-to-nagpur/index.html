Plywood Supply to Nagpur

Cochin Wood Industries

Why Nagpur sits at the centre of India's packing-plywood map

Nagpur is geographically the closest thing India has to a logistics centre of gravity — the so-called Zero Mile. Cargo here moves north to Delhi, south to Chennai, east to Kolkata and west to JNPT/Mumbai with broadly similar truck-day economics. That single fact reshapes what factories around the city ship and how they pack it. Aerospace MRO units at MIHAN need crates that pass airline cargo scrutiny. Engineering goods leaving Butibori MIDC go out on heavy trailers and need pallet decks that survive double-handling. Orange exporters out of Kalmeshwar and Katol need fumigated wooden boxes for sea legs to the Gulf. The Cochin Wood group supplies packing-grade plywood, crate kits, pallets and container-flooring boards into all four of those flows from our Perumbavoor base near Kochi.

The Nagpur industrial picture

Before we talk product, it helps to map the demand. Nagpur's plywood-packing demand is not one market — it is at least five, each with a different load profile.

MIHAN SEZ and the aerospace cluster

MIHAN (Multi-modal International Cargo Hub and Airport at Nagpur) was built around the air-cargo proposition, and the cluster around it now includes Boeing's MRO facility, Dassault Reliance Aerospace, TAL Manufacturing and a tier of avionics and component shops. Aerospace movements need crates with predictable moisture behaviour, ISPM-15 compliance for international legs, and tight tolerance on internal dunnage. Plywood thickness here is typically 12 mm or 18 mm for the case walls, with 18 mm or 25 mm bearers.

Butibori MIDC and Hingna MIDC

Butibori is one of Asia's larger industrial estates by area, and Hingna sits closer to the city. Between them you have heavy electrical equipment, transformers, auto components (Mahindra, Bajaj tier-two), steel fabrication and CNC machine builders. These are pallet-and-crate buyers in the literal sense — pallets to move work-in-progress on the shop floor, and heavy export crates for finished goods bound for JNPT or Mundra. Standard EUR-pallet (1200 x 800 mm) and ISO-pallet (1200 x 1000 mm) deck sizes dominate.

Orange and agri-export belt

The Vidarbha orange belt around Katol, Kalmeshwar, Narkhed and Warud ships fresh and processed produce out via Mumbai and Mundra to the GCC. Plywood boxes for export-grade fruit need light-weight, low-moisture panels that pass phytosanitary inspection.

Coal, mining and power equipment

Western Coalfields headquartered at Nagpur, and the surrounding thermal-power equipment trade, generates a quiet but steady stream of demand for shuttering ply (formwork during plant construction) and heavy-duty crating for rotating equipment.

Container flooring and trailer-body shops

The trailer and container-body fabricators along the Wardha Road and Kamptee Road belt buy 28 mm container-flooring panels by the lot.

Where the buyer wants a cleaner face — typically for export crates that will be inspected at the receiving port, or for fruit-export boxes where face-fibre matters — we ship Okoume-faced packing ply. Quote on request; this sits at a premium to the MR-Packing range.

We can ship loose plywood, or pre-cut kits, or finished pallets and crates. Standard pallet sizes built for Nagpur shops include 1200 x 800 mm (EUR), 1200 x 1000 mm (ISO), 1100 x 1100 mm (Asia) and bespoke heavy-duty decks for transformer and machine-tool loads. ISPM-15 heat-treatment is available on the wooden runners where the export leg requires it.

Container flooring

28 mm phenolic-faced container-flooring boards in standard 2440 x 1220 mm sheets for the trailer and container-body fabricators on Wardha Road.

Kochi to Nagpur — the logistics that decide the price

The Kochi-to-Nagpur leg is roughly 1,500 km by road. Realistic transit on a single-trailer movement is 4 to 5 days door-to-door, depending on whether the trailer routes via Bangalore-Hyderabad-Adilabad or via Mangalore-Pune-Aurangabad. We typically dispatch from Perumbavoor (north of Kochi, about 30 km from Kochi Port) on 32 ft or 40 ft trailers carrying 25 to 28 tonnes of plywood per load, which works out to roughly 750 to 900 sheets of 18 mm equivalent per trailer.

For Nagpur buyers, this means two things. First, full-trailer (FTL) economics dominate — a single buyer ordering one full load gets a meaningfully lower landed cost than a part-load. Second, lead time from PI to delivered material in Nagpur is usually 10 to 14 days, with 7 days for production and dispatch and 4 to 5 days in transit. We have also routed export-bound material via Kochi Port and JNPT when the final destination is the GCC, which often beats sending the finished crate back across India.

Decision rules — which grade fits which load

A short operator's heuristic for buyers in the city:

  • If the load is going by sea and will be inspected at destination — specify Okoume face on at least the visible panels. The cleaner face passes scrutiny better.
  • If the load is heavy machinery moving by road or rail within India — MR-Packing grade in 18 mm walls with 25 mm bearers is the standard call.
  • If the load is fruit going to the GCC via Mundra — 6 mm or 9 mm MR-Packing in pre-cut box kits, ISPM-15 stamp on the bearers, keeps cost-per-box defensible.
  • If the work is plant or substation construction at Butibori or Koradi — shuttering ply, not packing grade. Different specification, different price.
  • If the work is container or trailer body fabrication — 28 mm phenolic container-flooring, no substitute.

FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity for delivery to Nagpur?

For a direct trailer dispatch from Perumbavoor, the economics start to work at roughly one full trailer — 25 tonnes, or about 750 to 900 sheets of 18 mm equivalent. Smaller quantities are possible but the per-sheet freight share gets heavy. Many Nagpur buyers consolidate a quarter's requirement into one FTL.

Do you supply ISPM-15 certified packing material?

Yes — for export-bound crates and pallets, we supply with ISPM-15 heat-treatment stamps on the wooden runners, which clears most international ports including the GCC, Vietnam, US and EU receiving terminals.

How long does a quote take?

If we have the thickness, sheet size, total quantity and a Nagpur delivery pincode, a firm PI usually goes back the same working day. Bespoke crate kits or pallet designs need 24 to 48 hours for the BOM.
