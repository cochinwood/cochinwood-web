Plywood Supply to Vadodara

Cochin Wood Industries

Vadodara sits at the crossroads of three industrial corridors that all generate serious packing volume: the petrochemical cluster around Koyali and Dumad, the pharma belt running north toward Halol and Savli, and the engineering and automotive footprint that has expanded along NH-48. Every drum, every reactor part, every vial-filled carton that leaves these plants needs a wooden case or pallet underneath it. That is the demand we ship into from Kochi. This page covers what we send, how we route it, and how a buyer in Gujarat should think about specifying plywood for the kind of cargo Vadodara typically moves.

The Vadodara industrial picture

Vadodara is one of the few Indian cities where you can drive twenty kilometres in three directions and hit three completely different export profiles. The packing requirements look quite different in each one.

Petrochemicals and chemicals

The Koyali refinery belt and the chemical units around Nandesari GIDC, Ranoli and Dumad generate steady demand for heavy-duty drum packing, skid bases for pumps and valves, and protective crates for catalyst and resin shipments. Drum collars, four-way pallets rated for 1,000 to 1,500 kg, and plywood-walled cases for dangerous-goods consignments are the typical asks.

Pharma and fine chemicals

The Halol GIDC corridor and the Savli industrial area host a dense pharma cluster — APIs, formulations, intermediates. Pharma export crates need to be clean, ISPM-15 stamped, and consistent in dimension because the cargo inside is high-value and temperature-sensitive. Repeat tenders for monthly export shipments through Mundra and JNPT are common from this belt.

Automotive and heavy engineering

Waghodia GIDC, Manjusar GIDC and the units along the Vadodara-Halol highway handle machined components, hydraulic assemblies and earth-moving spares. Loads run heavy and odd-shaped, so the packing usually means thicker plywood walls, internal bracing and custom skid bases rather than off-the-shelf box sizes.

Other estates worth naming

  • Makarpura GIDC — mixed engineering and ancillary units, regular flow of medium-weight crates.
  • Por-Ramangamdi — chemical and process units.
  • GIDC Vadodara (Gorwa) — older estate with mixed manufacturing.
  • Padra and Jambusar belt — petrochem expansion zone, growing pallet demand.

What we ship into Vadodara

The Cochin Wood group has been manufacturing plywood in Perumbavoor since 1986, and the packing-grade range is what we move the most of into Gujarat. Here is what typically goes onto a truck heading north.

Packing-grade plywood sheets

Our MR-Packing grade comes in thicknesses from 6 mm up to 18 mm in standard 8x4 sheets. Buyers in Vadodara usually want a working mix:

  • 6 mm and 8 mm — top and bottom panels of light pharma crates, internal partitions.
  • 10 mm to 12 mm — side walls of mid-weight export cases, drum collars.
  • 15 mm to 18 mm — base panels under heavy machinery, container flooring patches, skid construction.

The face veneer can be specified as commercial hardwood, rubberwood, or Okoume on the BWR variants where appearance and moisture tolerance matter for longer sea legs.

Finished crates and cases

For buyers who do not want to fabricate in-house, we supply ready-to-pack wooden cases built to ISPM-15 specification with the heat-treatment stamp. Standard footprints we make routinely: 1200x800 mm, 1200x1000 mm, and custom drum-cluster crates for petrochem shipments. Internal cleating and corner bracing are added on the bill of materials when the cargo asks for it.

Pallets

Four-way entry pallets in standard EUR (1200x800 mm) and ISO (1200x1000 mm) sizes, plus custom footprints for specific machine bases. Load ratings up to 1,500 kg static for the heavy-engineering work that comes out of Waghodia and Manjusar.

Container flooring

For exporters consolidating their own containers at the factory, we supply 28 mm container flooring plywood — apitong-equivalent hardwood-face panels in the standard 2400x1160 mm size. Useful for refurbishing older containers or stuffing one-trip shipments where the existing floor is suspect.

Kochi to Vadodara logistics

The road distance from our Perumbavoor dispatch yards to Vadodara is roughly 1,750 km. Two routes work, and we pick based on load size and timing.

For pharma buyers who run monthly shipments, a planned dispatch schedule keeps freight per sheet predictable and avoids spot-rate volatility.

Decision rules: which grade for which Vadodara load

Use MR-Packing grade when

  • The crate is going by road or short sea route to a domestic or GCC destination.
  • Cargo weight is under 2 tonnes per case.
  • The receiver does not require a hardwood-face cosmetic finish.

Use Okoume-faced BWR when

  • The shipment is going by sea to Europe, the US, or longer routes via Mundra.
  • The cargo is pharma export or high-value chemical product where the receiving auditor scrutinises packing material.
  • Multiple humidity cycles are expected in transit.

Use shuttering ply when

  • Buyer is on the construction side — civil contractors building new factory blocks in Halol or Savli often pull shuttering ply alongside packing material on the same truck.
  • The application is concrete formwork rather than packing.

FAQ

Do you supply ISPM-15 heat-treated crates for export shipments out of Mundra and JNPT?

Yes. All export crates carry the ISPM-15 heat-treatment stamp from an approved facility. The compliance certificate travels with the dispatch documents so the freight forwarder can clear customs without delays.

What is the minimum order quantity for a direct dispatch to Vadodara?

For sheets, we prefer to load a full truck (14 to 16 tonnes) because part-load freight from Kerala to Gujarat eats into the unit economics. For finished crates and pallets, the minimum depends on the spec — small batches of custom crates can ride with a sheet load to the same destination.

How are prices quoted — delivered Vadodara or ex-Perumbavoor?

Both, depending on what the buyer prefers. Our standard price card is ex-factory at Perumbavoor with GST and freight extra. For repeat customers we also issue delivered Vadodara quotes that lock in the trucking lane, which makes monthly budgeting easier.
