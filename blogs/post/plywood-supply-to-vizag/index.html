Plywood Supply to Vizag

Cochin Wood Industries

Why source from Kochi

Packaging supply for JNPC pharma, RINL steel and Vizag's seafood belt.

JNPC pharma exporters palletise daily for FDA-audited buyers in the US, EU and Japan — cold-chain, ISPM-15 stamped, dimension-fixed for IBC/HDPE drum loads. RINL Vizag Steel ships coils, plates and pipes that need heavy plywood cases and skid bases for trans-shipment. The seafood belt (less plywood-intensive) palletises frozen cartons inside reefer containers. APSEZ Visakhapatnam SEZ at Atchutapuram and Brandix India Apparel City add their own export volume. Vizag Port (containers via VCTPL, plus 10 CFS) and Gangavaram Port (Adani, deep-draft) handle the throughput. We hold the Kochi-origin supply line into that belt.

Logistics

Kochi to Vizag — 1,400-1,500 km via NH-544/NH-44/NH-16.

The route runs Kochi-Salem-Bengaluru-Hyderabad-Vijayawada-Visakhapatnam — about 1,400-1,500 km, 30-36 hours of truck transit. For full-truck-equivalent volumes, rail container via Vijayawada or coastal shipping ex-Cochin Port are cost-efficient alternatives. Vizag's coastal position means most cargo moves direct port-side: drop at your JNPC facility or onward to Vizag Port (VCTPL/CFS) or Gangavaram CFS. For exports we supply ISPM-15 heat-treated, IPPC-stamped wood with phytosanitary documentation, and original certificates released to verified buyers on request — including USFDA-acceptable plant-quarantine documentation for pharma.

Andhra Pradesh corridor

We also serve Sri City, Tirupati EMC and the Krishnapatnam corridor.

Vizag is one half of our AP coverage; the southern half is the Sri City · Tirupati · Krishnapatnam belt. Sri City is South India's largest greenfield integrated industrial city — 7,500 acres, 240+ companies from 31 countries, ₹65,000 crore cumulative exports by August 2025, with 6,000 additional acres announced for expansion. Tirupati Electronics Manufacturing Cluster (EMC) at Renigunta drives the handset and display-panel packaging volumes. Krishnapatnam Port (Adani deep-draft) is the nearest gateway; Chennai, Kattupalli and Ennore are all within ~100 km, giving the corridor four deep-water ports inside a one-hour radius. Kochi-to-Sri-City road run is 750-800 km, 16-20 hr via NH-544/NH-44/NH-716. Same Vizag desk handles enquiries; ask for "AP south corridor".

Vizag FAQ

Common questions from Vizag buyers

Are your ISPM-15 certificates USFDA-acceptable for JNPC pharma exports?

Yes. USDA-APHIS-recognised IPPC heat-treatment stamps + phytosanitary certificates satisfy USFDA, EMA and equivalent agency requirements. Original certificates released to verified buyers on request.

Can you deliver to Vizag Port or Gangavaram CFS?

Yes — both Vizag Port (VCTPL + CFS belt) and Gangavaram Port (Adani) are regular drops. Trucks dispatched from Kochi reach in 30-36 hours; coastal shipping ex-Cochin Port also available for bulk pallet consignments.

Is the wood ISPM-15 compliant for export?

Export pallets and crates are heat-treated and IPPC-stamped to ISPM-15, with phytosanitary documentation for the destination country.

What is the minimum order?

We are a bulk supplier and quote on container or full-truck volumes. Share your monthly requirement and we will confirm terms.
