Plywood Supply to Pune

Cochin Wood Industries

Heavy-duty plywood for Pune's forgings, engines and SKD lines.

Chakan-Talegaon turns out roughly 40% of India's passenger vehicles, with a major new auto line commissioned at Talegaon in October 2025. The Mundhwa-Khed forging belt ships crankshafts and aerospace forgings to North America, Europe and the Gulf. Add Pune's heavy-engineering and pump-and-boiler exporters across Pimpri-Chinchwad, Khed City and Ranjangaon — this region crates at scale and to spec. We supply Kochi-origin packing-grade plywood, heavy-duty skids and bespoke crates into that export market.

Forging-load skids·ISPM-15 heat-treated·Kochi → ICD Talegaon / Dighi

Packaging that holds up to Chakan and the Khed forging belt.

From the Chakan-Akurdi auto belt and the Pimpri-Chinchwad tier-1 component cluster to the Khed City defence-forging zone and the Ranjangaon EMC, Pune is one of India's biggest sources of high-value export cargo — crankshafts to North America and Europe, motorcycles to Africa and Latin America, EV battery packs to ASEAN. The packaging that ships them runs heavy: 18mm BWR ply skids with hardwood bearers under forgings, returnable plywood pallets for CKD/SKD auto lines, closed plywood boxes for engines and pumps. We supply Kochi-origin plywood and crating into the Pune belt — delivered to your facility or routed directly to ICD Talegaon (INTLG6) or ICD Dighi (INDIG6) for stuffing onto JNPT lines.

Kochi to Pune — 1,400 km, three days door-to-door.

All road. The fastest line runs inland on NH-544/NH-44 through Salem, Bengaluru, Hubli and Kolhapur (about 1,250 km); the coastal NH-66 via Mangaluru-Karwar-Panaji works for monsoon detours. A 32-ft trailer loaded at our yard typically arrives in 2.5-3 days, or directly at ICD Talegaon / ICD Dighi if you are stuffing for JNPT. For overseas cargo we supply ISPM-15 heat-treated, IPPC-stamped wood with phytosanitary documentation, and original certificates released to verified buyers on request. Share your destination port, skid spec and monthly volume — we will quote ex-Kochi with transport to Pune costed in.

Common questions from Pune buyers

Do you build heavy-duty skids for forging exports?

Yes. Our heavy-load skid build for crankshaft and axle exports is 18mm BWR ply on hardwood bearers, ISPM-15 IPPC-stamped. Send weights and bearer spacing — we will quote the skid.

Can you deliver directly to ICD Talegaon or ICD Dighi?

Yes — KSH Distriparks Talegaon (INTLG6) and CONCOR Dighi (INDIG6) are regular drop points. Trucks dispatched from Kochi reach Pune in 2.5-3 days; stuffing can be timed to your container booking.

Is the wood ISPM-15 compliant for export?

Export pallets and crates are heat-treated and IPPC-stamped to ISPM-15, with phytosanitary documentation for the destination country.

What is the minimum order?

We are a bulk supplier and quote on container or full-truck volumes. Share your monthly requirement and we will confirm terms.
