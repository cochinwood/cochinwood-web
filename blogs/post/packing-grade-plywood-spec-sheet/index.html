Everything you need to specify and order packing & export plywood · Cochin Wood Industries

How to order in four steps: choose your grade (MR, BWR or BWP), choose your face (commercial hardwood or Okoume), pick your thickness and sheet size, then send us four details and we'll quote — same day, FOB Cochin.

1 · Choose your grade

The grade is set by the adhesive bond — how much water the panel will face in transit and storage. See BWP vs BWR explained for the full detail.

GradeStandardWater resistanceBest for
MR — Moisture-ResistantIS 303Resists humidityThe workhorse — dry road, rail & container export cases
BWR — Boiling-Water-ResistantIS 303HighHumid ports, monsoon transit, longer storage
BWP — MarineIS 710WaterproofWater-exposed cargo / long sea voyages (over-spec for dry crating)

2 · Choose your face

Face veneerWeightSurfaceBest for
Commercial hardwoodStandardStandardEconomical — the default for most cases
Okoume (gaboon)LightSmooth, paintableLower freight + clean branding; export cases where weight matters

3 · Common thicknesses & sizes

Thicknesses: 4, 6, 9, 12, 16, 19 and 25 mm (other thicknesses on request).

Sheet sizes: 8'×4' standard; custom sizes (e.g. 10'×5') made to order.

4 · ISPM-15, in one line

Plywood is generally exempt from ISPM-15 (manufacturing eliminates pests). The solid-wood framing and runners are heat-treated and stamped. We supply finished cases and crates already ISPM-15 compliant for any country that enforces it. See ISPM-15 for plywood packaging.
