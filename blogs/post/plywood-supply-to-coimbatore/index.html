Plywood Supply to Coimbatore

Cochin Wood Industries

One-day delivery from Kochi to Coimbatore's pump, machinery and knitwear floors.

Coimbatore makes about 40% of India's pumps. LMW at Arasur is one of three global makers of full-range spinning machinery. The city holds the GI-tagged wet-grinder industry. And Tiruppur, 55 km east, did roughly ₹40,000 crore in knitwear exports in FY25. From our Kochi yard, this whole belt is a single drive through the Palakkad Gap — about 190 km, six hours, often same-day delivery. We supply Coimbatore's export market with packing-grade plywood, machine-tool crates and knitwear pallets in volume.

Why source from Kochi

Packaging supply for India's Pump City and the Tiruppur knitwear belt.

Coimbatore's pump and compressor exporters ship to over 100 countries. The textile-machinery cluster at Arasur and Periyanaickenpalayam crates spinning-frame modules at 2-6 tonnes apiece. Wet-grinder exporters in Singanallur and Peelamedu fill containers for diaspora B2B in the US and Gulf. The auto-component JIT belt at Sulur and Ganapathy ships returnable plywood bins. Tiruppur knitwear stuffs containers at pallet volume. ICD Irugur (INIGU6) handles daily rail stuffing for Tuticorin and Chennai. Coimbatore is the closest major industrial city to Kochi — close enough to drop today and deliver tomorrow.

Logistics

Kochi to Coimbatore — six hours through the Palakkad Gap.

Coimbatore is the closest major city to our Kochi yard — about 190 km on NH-544 via Aluva, Thrissur, the Palakkad Gap and the Walayar border. A 32-ft trailer dispatched at 6 AM is unloading in Coimbatore by lunch. No ghats, no detours — the Palakkad Gap is the only flat road through the Western Ghats. For overseas cargo we supply ISPM-15 heat-treated, IPPC-stamped wood with phytosanitary documentation, and original certificates released to verified buyers on request. Same-day quote, next-day delivery: share monthly volume and stuffing yard (Irugur ICD, Tuticorin, Chennai or in-house) and we will quote ex-Kochi.

Coimbatore FAQ

Common questions from Coimbatore buyers

How fast can you deliver to Coimbatore?

Same day for orders dispatched by 6 AM. Coimbatore is 190 km from our Kochi yard — a six-to-seven hour drive through the Palakkad Gap on NH-544. Standard: next morning.

Can you supply for stuffing at ICD Irugur?

Yes — Irugur (INIGU6) is a regular drop. We can time delivery to your stuffing slot for onward rail to Tuticorin or Chennai.

Do you handle small bracing runs for wet-grinder exporters?

Yes. Smaller braced-carton runs for wet-grinder boxes fit alongside larger pump or machinery orders — share monthly count and we combine on a single dispatch.

Is the wood ISPM-15 compliant for export?

Export pallets and crates are heat-treated and IPPC-stamped to ISPM-15, with phytosanitary documentation for the destination country.

Shipping out of Coimbatore or Tiruppur?

Send size, grade and monthly volume — same-day quote, next-day delivery from Kochi.
