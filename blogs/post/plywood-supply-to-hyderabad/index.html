Plywood Supply to Hyderabad

Cochin Wood Industries

FDA-grade plywood for Hyderabad's bulk-drug, vaccine, aerospace and Pharma City exporters.

Hyderabad ships about $5.9 billion in pharma exports a year — roughly 18% of India's total — from 214 USFDA-approved facilities. Telangana supplies about 65% of India's vaccine output. The GMR Aerospace SEZ at Shamshabad hosts multiple OEM joint ventures. And Pharma City at Yadadri — 19,000 acres, the world's largest planned pharma cluster — has 150+ anchors signed for Phase 1. Hyderabad's packaging demand is specialised: FDA-traceable, cleanroom-suitable, defence-spec. We supply Kochi-origin plywood and crating into that market, 970 km by road.

Why source from Kochi

Packaging supply for Genome Valley, Pharma City and the GMR Aerospace SEZ.

Hyderabad's bulk-drug and formulation exporters — concentrated around Pashamylaram, Patancheru, Jeedimetla and Bonthapally — palletise daily for FDA-audited buyers in the US, EU and Latin America. We supply Kochi-origin plywood into that market.

Logistics

Kochi to Hyderabad — 970 km, two days on NH-544 / NH-44.

The route runs Kochi-Salem-Bengaluru-Anantapur-Kurnool-Hyderabad on NH-544 then NH-44 — about 970 km, two-day truck transit for a 32-ft trailer. Loads drop at your facility or route directly to ICD Sanathnagar (CONCOR's South-Central flagship) for stuffing onto rail to Krishnapatnam, Visakhapatnam or Chennai Port. For exporters who consolidate plywood with Kerala hardwood runners for in-house crate assembly, we ship both on one truck. For overseas cargo we supply ISPM-15 heat-treated, IPPC-stamped wood with phytosanitary documentation, and original certificates released to verified buyers on request — including USFDA-acceptable plant-quarantine documentation. Share destination port and monthly volume.

Hyderabad FAQ

Common questions from Hyderabad buyers

Are your ISPM-15 certificates USFDA-acceptable?

Yes. We supply USDA-APHIS-recognised IPPC heat-treatment stamps and phytosanitary certificates that satisfy USFDA, EMA and equivalent agency requirements for pharma exports. Original certificates released to verified buyers on request.

Can you supply low-moisture ply for vaccines or aerospace?

Yes. Cold-chain-validated, sub-12% moisture-content plywood is part of our standard offering for vaccine and aerospace export use. Specify moisture target — we will document it on the cert.

Can you deliver to ICD Sanathnagar?

Yes. ICD Sanathnagar (CONCOR's South-Central zone hub, 3,000-TEU stack) is a regular drop. Trucks from Kochi reach in two days; stuffing can be timed to your rail schedule.

Is the wood ISPM-15 compliant for export?

Export pallets and crates are heat-treated and IPPC-stamped to ISPM-15, with phytosanitary documentation for the destination country.

Crating from Pharma City, Genome Valley or the GMR SEZ?

Send spec, moisture target and destination port — we will quote ex-Kochi with delivery to Hyderabad or ICD Sanathnagar.
