Plywood Supply to Davangere

Cochin Wood Industries

Davangere sits in the dead centre of Karnataka, on NH-48 between Bengaluru and Hubballi, and it is one of those second-tier industrial cities that quietly does a lot of physical work — cotton ginning, spinning and weaving on one side, agri-processing and farm machinery on the other. Both of those activities push finished goods out of Davangere on trucks, and a fair share of that cargo travels in wooden boxes, on wooden pallets, or inside containers laid with plywood flooring. That is where a Kochi-based packing-grade plywood supply starts to make economic sense for Davangere buyers, and it is what this guide breaks down.

The Davangere industrial picture

Davangere is historically the cotton-textile capital of Karnataka. The Bapuji Industrial Estate inside city limits, the KSSIDC industrial area at Lokikere Road, the Karur and Anagodu cluster on the Harihar side, and the larger KIADB belts at Harihar and Haralapura form the spine of the local manufacturing base. Around the edges, the Davangere–Harihar corridor on NH-48 carries a dense run of cotton mills, ginning units, oil mills, foundries and agri-equipment fabricators.

The plywood demand that flows out of this belt is fairly predictable:

  • Textile machinery and spares — ring frames, autoconers, looms and their replacement parts move in heavy wooden crates, often inter-state, and need square, dense ply that holds nails without splitting.
  • Agri-equipment and pump-set packing — irrigation pumps, sprayer assemblies, threshers and tractor spares are crated for dealer dispatch across South and Central India.
  • Cotton bales and yarn cartons on pallets — finished yarn, processed cotton and ginned lint move on wooden pallets to mills, ports and inland warehouses.
  • Foundry castings and engineered components — Davangere has a long-standing foundry cluster; iron castings need heavy-duty box bottoms and skidded crates.
  • Export-bound agri-processed goods — areca-nut, maize products and processed grains routed via Mangaluru, Krishnapatnam or Chennai ports, where ISPM-15-compliant crating becomes a factor.

The common thread: durable, predictable, fairly thick ply in 6 mm–18 mm with consistent quality across truckloads. Not retail-counter ply.

What we ship into Davangere

The Cochin Wood group has been manufacturing plywood out of Perumbavoor since 1986 — about forty years of continuous operations — and CWI Pvt Ltd today supplies pan-India out of contract factories built to group specifications. For Davangere, the typical stack is:

Packing-grade plywood (the workhorse)

Commercial / rubberwood packing-grade ply is what most Davangere crate-makers and pallet shops actually need. It is engineered for nail-holding strength, dimensional stability and a consistent thickness — not for furniture polish. We ship it in standard 8' x 4' sheets across the packing thickness range:

Wooden crates and packing cases

For buyers who would rather receive finished crates than build them in-house, the group also supplies fabricated boxes — closed, slatted, or skidded — built around the customer's product drawing. Typical Davangere builds: 1.2 m x 0.8 m x 1.0 m machinery crates with 12 mm walls and runner skids, or larger 2.0 m boxes for full ring-frame assemblies.

Pallets

Standard four-way entry pallets in 1200 x 1000 mm and 1200 x 800 mm (Euro), plus custom footprints for cotton bale stacking, are built from sawn timber decks or plywood tops with hardwood runners.

Container flooring and base boards

18 mm packing ply in 8' x 4' sheets is the common floor liner for ODC and break-bulk container loads moving from inland Davangere down to Mangaluru or across to Chennai. It protects the container metal floor and gives forklifts a clean working surface.

Okoume plywood

Okoume is a premium export-grade plywood we keep separately. For most Davangere domestic packing, it is overkill. It earns its price when the buyer is shipping high-value finished goods — engineered textile machinery for export, precision spares, electronics — where surface finish, light weight and long sea-freight life matter.

Shuttering plywood and sawn timber

Film-faced shuttering ply for the construction trade and rough sawn timber for runners and skids round out the supply.

Kochi-to-Davangere logistics

The road distance from Kochi/Ernakulam to Davangere is roughly 580–620 km, depending on whether the truck routes via Salem–Bengaluru on NH-44/NH-48 or via Mysuru on NH-766 and NH-275. The cleaner heavy-truck lane is the Salem–Bengaluru–Tumakuru–Chitradurga–Davangere run on NH-48, which is the standard six-lane corridor.

  • Transit time: a fully loaded part-load or full truck typically reaches Davangere in 24–36 hours from dispatch, weather and check-posts permitting.
  • Truck type: 32-ft single-axle or 22-ft container trucks are the practical sizes; the 32-ft handles a full pallet/crate load comfortably with room for top-stacked ply sheets.
  • Documentation: e-way bill from Kerala (origin) to Karnataka (destination) is standard; GST is charged IGST since it is an inter-state movement.
  • Port option: for the rare buyer who would rather receive at a Karnataka port, Mangaluru is the closer one (~250 km from Davangere). Most Davangere buyers find direct road from Kochi simpler and cheaper than the port detour.

Decision rules — which grade for which Davangere load

A practical shortlist that we walk every new Davangere buyer through before quoting:

  1. Domestic crate for a textile machine or agri pump → commercial packing ply, 12–16 mm walls, 18 mm base. Don't pay for Okoume.
  2. Pallet for cotton bales or yarn cartons → 12 mm packing ply deck on hardwood runners, or full sawn-timber deck. Pick by weight and re-use cycle.
  3. Container floor liner for ODC load to a port → 18 mm packing ply, full 8' x 4' sheets, laid edge-to-edge.
  4. Export crate for engineered textile machinery → Okoume face ply if the customer specifies a clean export look, ISPM-15 heat-treated softwood frame, IPPC stamp.
  5. Shuttering for a Davangere construction site → film-faced shuttering ply, separate price list, ordered by repetition count needed.

When in doubt, share the product dimensions, gross weight, dispatch mode (truck vs container vs port) and monthly off-take. We will revert with a thickness recommendation and a price per sheet or per finished crate.

FAQ

What is the minimum order to Davangere?

Practical minimum is a part-load of about 8–10 tonnes (roughly 250–300 sheets of mid-thickness packing ply, or a mixed crate-and-pallet consignment). Smaller trial orders are possible but the freight per sheet becomes uneconomical below that range.

Can you cut sheets to crate sizes before dispatch?

Yes. Repeat crate orders are usually cheaper to cut at our end and ship as a kit — the buyer saves on local cutting labour and waste. Share the BOM with grain direction and we will quote both ways.

Do you offer GST credit-invoice and e-way bill?

Every consignment ships with a proforma upfront, a tax invoice on dispatch (IGST for Karnataka), and a Kerala-to-Karnataka e-way bill. No cash, no kachcha bills.
