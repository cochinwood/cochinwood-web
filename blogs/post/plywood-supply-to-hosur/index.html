Plywood Supply to Hosur

Cochin Wood Industries

Hosur is one of the few inland Indian cities where almost every truck rolling out of an industrial gate carries something packed in wood. The stretch from SIPCOT Phase I down to the Bengaluru-Krishnagiri NH-44 spine moves auto components, two-wheeler sub-assemblies, watch movements, machine tools, defense electronics and Caterpillar earth-moving spares — and every one of those loads needs a crate, a pallet or a plywood-decked container floor under it. That demand profile is exactly what our Kochi-based packing operation is built around, and Hosur sits on a clean 370 km trucking lane from us via NH-544 and NH-44.

The Hosur industrial picture

Hosur is not one estate, it is a corridor. Buyers placing plywood and crate orders typically sit in one of these belts:

  • SIPCOT Hosur Phase I and Phase II — the original anchor estates housing TVS group units, Ashok Leyland's plant, Caterpillar India's earth-moving operation, Titan watches, and a dense ecosystem of Tier-1 and Tier-2 auto component suppliers feeding all of them.
  • Mookandapalli, Bagalur Road and Belagondapalli industrial clusters — sheet-metal pressing, forging, plastic moulding and electronics assembly units that ship to Bengaluru OEMs every day.
  • Thally Road / Berigai stretch — newer warehousing and contract-manufacturing capacity that has spilled over from Bengaluru and Whitefield as land there got expensive.
  • Defense and aerospace electronics — a small but high-value pocket of vendors building boxed sub-assemblies that ship under ISPM-15 wood-packaging rules.

The packaging brief is fairly consistent across these segments: heavy, dimensionally awkward, often export-bound, and zero tolerance for moisture damage or pest rejection at a foreign port. That is a plywood problem, not a corrugated problem.

What we ship to Hosur

Most Hosur enquiries that land with us fall into four categories. Here is what we typically quote.

1. Packing-grade plywood (sheets)

This is the workhorse — commercial / rubberwood plywood used by crate fabricators and in-house packing shops in 6 mm up to 18 mm thicknesses. The standard Hosur sheet sizes are 8x4 ft and 7x4 ft, with 12 mm and 18 mm being the volume runners for auto-component crates. We ship ex-factory from Perumbavoor; GST and transport are extra. Buyers typically take a full truck (about 600-650 sheets of 12 mm) for a 9-11 hour transit straight into SIPCOT.

2. ISPM-15 stamped crates and cases

For export-bound shipments — particularly auto components heading to ASEAN and GCC, and electronics moving to Europe — the wood-packaging material must carry the ISPM-15 heat-treatment stamp. Crate construction we cut for the Hosur belt usually combines an 18 mm packing-ply skin over a hardwood (jackwood or rubberwood) battened frame, with corner reinforcements sized to the part being shipped. Engineering drawings or a sample part are enough for us to quote.

3. Wooden pallets

Standard sizes that move well in Hosur are 1200x1000 mm (the Euro-friendly footprint many auto OEMs default to) and 1200x800 mm. Two-way and four-way entry, hardwood deck boards over a stringer or block base. We also build heavy-duty pallets rated for 1.5-2 tonne static loads where forging units and machine-tool exporters need them.

4. Container flooring plywood

Less common but a real Hosur ask — 28 mm container-floor grade ply for buyers refurbishing reefers and dry containers, and for OEMs building purpose-fitted intermodal boxes. Quoted on spec, not a stock line.

Kochi to Hosur — the logistics

The route that works for most of our Hosur shipments:

  • Distance: roughly 370 km from Perumbavoor / Kochi to SIPCOT Hosur
  • Route: NH-544 from Kochi through Salem, then NH-44 north toward Krishnagiri and Hosur
  • Transit: 9-11 hours on a single-driver run; an overnight despatch from us is typically unloading at the Hosur gate by mid-morning the next day
  • Vehicle types: 32 ft single-axle and multi-axle trailers for full-truck plywood; container movements where the customer needs a sealed load

For buyers who prefer it, we can also route via Coimbatore-Salem-Krishnagiri (effectively the same NH-544/NH-44 spine). Cochin Port is in play only for export consignments where the Hosur shipper is exporting through Kochi rather than Chennai — a small but real share of our enquiries.

Which grade for which Hosur load

A practical rule we work with, after years of quoting this belt:

Typical loadRecommended materialWhy
Auto components, domestic dispatch (Bengaluru, Chennai, Pune)12-15 mm commercial packing ply on hardwood frameStrength-to-cost optimum, no export grade premium needed
Auto / electronics export to ASEAN, GCC, EU15-18 mm packing ply, ISPM-15 stamped, hardwood battensPhytosanitary compliance + handling-shock margin
High-value or sensitive electronics, defense sub-assembliesOkoume-faced ply on selected panelsCleaner finish, lower internal abrasion, premium presentation
Site-cast civil works, warehousing build-outsFilm-faced shuttering plyRepeat-pour reusability, smooth concrete finish

Okoume is a face-veneer option layered over our MR-packing or BWR construction — not a separate grade. When a Hosur exporter is presenting a crate at a foreign customer's receiving bay and wants it to look like a finished product, that is where Okoume earns its premium. For 90% of in-country auto-component movement, our standard commercial packing ply is the right answer and Okoume is overkill.

FAQ

What is your minimum order size for Hosur?

For packing-grade plywood we generally quote full-truck loads to Hosur to keep freight-per-sheet sensible — around 600-650 sheets of 12 mm or equivalent. Crate and pallet orders are quoted by job; even 100-200 units of a defined SKU is workable if drawings are clear.

Can you handle ISPM-15 stamping?

Yes. We supply heat-treated, ISPM-15 marked wood-packaging material where the buyer is exporting. The treatment certificate travels with the consignment.

What is the lead time from order confirmation?

Standard packing-grade plywood in volume runners (12 mm, 18 mm) typically dispatches within 5-7 working days of confirmation and advance. Custom crate jobs depend on drawings — usually 10-14 days for a first production run, faster on repeats. Hosur transit adds the 9-11 hour drive on top.
