Five sectors · five continents

Where our wood does its work.

Five sectors served from our Perumbavoor operation — to the spec, on the schedule, container after container. Each one below links straight to the grades it uses.

01 · Construction & Formwork

Panels that strip clean and come back for the next pour.

Civil contractors and formwork suppliers don't buy a sheet — they buy a number of pours. We make high-density film-faced shuttering in the film weights and thicknesses that survive repeat use and strip to a fair face, plus chequered anti-skid board for scaffold platforms and walkways. Sized to your form system, loaded by the container, the same panel each delivery.

02 · Marine & Boatbuilding

A bond that holds underwater, a core with no voids.

Boatbuilders and marine fit-out shops need plywood that survives immersion, steam and humidity without delaminating. The IS 710 BWP marine plywood we make is 100% phenolic-bonded on a full hardwood core — BS 1088 grade available on request — for hulls, decks, bulkheads and wet interiors. Boatyards supplied from Perumbavoor, backed by four decades of group manufacturing, cut to the yard's spec.

03 · Joinery & Interiors

Flat, predictable panels that take a finish.

Joinery firms, furniture clusters and interior contractors need the right grade for the right job — and the same board every order. We make commercial plywood in BWR and MR, block board, finger-joint board, particle board and sawn timber, gap-free and machined flat, so cabinets, carcasses and worktops come out true.

04 · Doors & Shutters

Shutters that hang true and hold a hinge.

Developers and door dealers need flush doors that stay flat and take a fixing without blowing out. The block-core flush doors we build come veneered, laminated or PU-finished, in standard residential sizes or bespoke dimensions — finished, crated and loaded for the container.

05 · Export Packaging

Packaging that clears customs and protects the cargo.

Exporters, OEMs and 3PLs crating heavy or engineering goods need packaging built to the cargo, not to a catalogue. We build ISPM-15 pallets, plywood boxes and crates and cable drums to your bill of materials — plus lightweight okoume packing plywood that takes weight, and freight, off every case.
