Questions buyers ask

Particle board, answered.

What is particle board used for?

Dry interior cabinetry, low-load shelving and modular or knock-down furniture — wherever a flat, cost-effective panel is needed and water exposure is low.

What is the difference between plain and pre-laminated?

Plain board you laminate or veneer yourself; pre-laminated arrives finished one or both sides in a décor, ready to cut.

Particle board or plywood — which should I use?

Particle board is lighter and cheaper for dry, low-load furniture; plywood is stronger, holds screws better and tolerates moisture. Match the board to the load and the environment.

What thickness and minimum order?

9–25 mm at 1220 × 2440 mm, supplied by the container load (FCL) FOB Cochin.
