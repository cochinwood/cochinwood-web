About

Cochin Wood Industries is a Kochi (Ernakulam) based plywood manufacturer and exporter, backed by a wood-manufacturing group operating since 1986. We supply packing-grade commercial and rubberwood plywood, Okoume plywood, shuttering ply, marine plywood, block board and sawn timber to packing-case makers, exporters and construction buyers across India and overseas, FOB Cochin.

Products

  • Commercial / packing-grade plywood (BWR & MR)
  • Marine plywood (IS 710 BWP)
  • Okoume plywood
  • Block board & flush doors
  • Plywood boxes & crates
  • Plywood pallets
  • Plywood cable drums
  • Sawn timber
