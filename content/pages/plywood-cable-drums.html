Questions buyers ask

Plywood cable drums, answered.

What flange sizes do you make?

Plywood cable drums from 600 mm to 3000 mm flange diameter, with the barrel and traverse sized to your cable.

Which cable types are they for?

Power, control, telecom and fibre-optic cable — the barrel diameter is set to respect your cable's minimum bend radius.

Do cable drums need ISPM-15?

The solid-wood arbor and members are heat-treated and stamped to ISPM-15 for export; the plywood flanges are exempt. We issue the certificate.

What is the minimum order and how do you ship?

Container load (FCL), FOB Cochin Port, painted and marked to your specification.
