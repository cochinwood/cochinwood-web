Questions buyers ask

Plywood pallets, answered.

Do plywood pallets need ISPM-15 treatment?

The plywood parts do not — plywood is an engineered wood and is ISPM-15 exempt. Any solid-wood blocks or bearers in the pallet must be heat-treated and stamped. We supply the treatment and the certificate. Full ISPM-15 explainer →

What sizes and entry types do you make?

Two-way and four-way, in 1200 × 1000, 1165 × 1165 and 1200 × 800 (Euro), or fully custom to your footprint and stacking pattern.

What load classes can you rate to?

Light, medium and heavy, with static and dynamic ratings specified to your payload — we build the deck, blocks and bearers to suit.

What is the minimum order and how do you ship?

One container load (FCL), FOB Cochin Port, with ISPM-15 documentation ready at dispatch.
