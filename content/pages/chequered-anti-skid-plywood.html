Questions buyers ask

Chequered anti-skid plywood, answered.

What is chequered anti-skid plywood?

Plywood with a pressed chequer or wire-mesh phenolic film that gives an anti-slip surface, on a waterproof phenolic core — used where people and wheels move over the panel.

Where is it used?

Container floors, scaffold platforms, bus and trailer floors, vehicle decks and walkways — anywhere grip and water resistance both matter.

How is it different from container flooring plywood?

It shares the anti-slip film, but container flooring is a denser 28 mm board graded to IICL TB-001 for structural container floors. See container flooring →

What thickness and minimum order?

12–28 mm at 1220 × 2440 mm or custom, supplied by the container load (FCL) FOB Cochin.
