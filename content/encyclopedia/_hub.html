Wood Encyclopedia

The wood species behind plywood, packing & timber

A working reference from the Cochin Wood Industries desk · independently researched, cross-checked and cited

What this is: a plain, honest reference to the woods we and our trade actually use — the veneers inside plywood, the hardwoods behind packing cases and pallets, and the commercial timbers of India. Each entry covers density, hardness, workability, durability, sustainability and real-world use, researched across multiple independent sources and cross-checked, with references on every page. The writing is our own.
