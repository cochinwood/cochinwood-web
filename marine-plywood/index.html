Questions buyers ask

Marine plywood, answered.

What is IS 710 marine plywood?

IS 710 is the Indian Standard for BWP (boiling-waterproof) marine plywood — a 100% phenolic-bonded board with a hardwood core that resists water, steam and humidity without delaminating.

What is the difference between IS 710 and IS 303?

IS 710 is boiling-waterproof marine grade; IS 303 is moisture-resistant commercial grade. Different glue, different water resistance, different use. Full comparison →

What thickness should I use for a boat?

It depends on the part — thin for bending, thicker for decks and bulkheads. See the thickness guide →

Do you supply BS 1088 and what is the minimum order?

BS 1088 grade is available on request. Supply is by the container load (FCL), FOB Cochin Port.
