Questions buyers ask

Plywood boxes & crates, answered.

Do plywood crates need ISPM-15 treatment?

The plywood panels are ISPM-15 exempt because plywood is processed wood. The solid-timber frame, skids and battens must be heat-treated and stamped — which we do, with certificate. Read the full ISPM-15 guide →

Can the crates be collapsible?

Yes — we build clip-assembled and fully collapsible knock-down crates that ship flat and re-assemble on site, cutting return freight and storage.

What is the maximum size and weight you can crate?

Bespoke, including heavy and over-dimensional cargo — we size the panels, frame and skid to the weight and centre of gravity you give us.

Do you provide cushioning and moisture protection?

Yes — cushioning, VCI/barrier lining, desiccant and bracing are specified to the cargo and the journey.
